Left behind

  • Bold, italic, colours, custom fonts
  • Audio & video ([sound:…])
  • MathJax / LaTeX stays as raw source
  • Tags, deck name, scheduling
  • Notion HTML / Markdown: only <details> toggles become cards — non-toggle blocks are ignored
  • Notion CSV (database alone): first two columns only; header row and extra columns ignored

Kept through

  • Cloze {{c1::…}} unwrapped to the answer text
  • Line breaks & <br> kept
  • Notion toggles: summary → front, contents → back; nested toggles → separate cards
  • Multi‑page Notion .zip exports unpacked recursively

Why some types don’t work

  • .colpkg is a full Anki profile backup (often multi‑GB). Export current deck as .apkg instead.
  • Notion PDF flattens toggles — use HTML, Markdown, or CSV.
